Blosxom vs the world

06 May 2002

In case you’re wondering why I’m using Blosxom instead of other fine weblogging tools like Radio Userland, Blogger, or Moveable Type, I’ll tell you. Not because I don’t want to pay the minimal (or non-existent) price; not because I think that one is better than the other, not even because I refuse free hosting for my weblog…

It’s simply because I can.
