SAP TechEd 2020 Channel 1 at home

04 Dec 2020 | 1 min read

I'll be watching all the Channel 1 content as it happens from the comfort of my TV - as well as interacting with individual sessions on my computer. Here's how.

Next week's SAP TechEd is almost upon us, and I'm looking forward to it, especially Channel 1, which you can learn more about in today's episode of SAP Developer News.

While I'll be interacting with individual sessions on my computer via the main SAP TechEd website I'm going to take advantage of the fact that the entire Channel 1 stream is going to be broadcast as a Twitter Live Event too, at this URL:

☞ https://twitter.com/i/events/1328768348725776384/

And best of all, it's ungated, i.e. you don't have to log on to anything to watch it!

So I'm going to set a Chrome browser tab up pointing to that Twitter Live Event URL, and then cast it to my TV via the power of Chromecast.

Chromecasting Channel 1 to my TV

That way I can enjoy the continuous content that Channel 1 will be offering -- learning from the hosts, moderators, guests & presenters in Keynote, Executive live Q&A, Strategy Talk and Live Interview sessions* -- as the sessions play out.

*you have registered for the Developer Keynote, right?

Anyway, let me know how you're going to watch Channel 1 on the Twitter Live Event stream. On the main living room TV? Audio only via your phone while walking? Something else? Let me know!
